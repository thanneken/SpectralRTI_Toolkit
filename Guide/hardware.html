Capture Hardware for SpectralRTI

This page is a stub for further development. The short version is that if you have a spectral imaging rig it is easy to add RTI. High quality cultural heritage spectral imaging equipment is available from MegaVision and PhaseOne. Lower-cost options come with tradeoffs, but the limiting factors pertain to spectral capture, not capture for RTI. Major options for adding RTI to a spectral rig are a fixed dome, a movable arc, or a hand-held flash.
